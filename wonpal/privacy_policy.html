Privacy Policy

Wonpal

How we handle your data — spoiler: we don't.

Last Updated: March 24, 2026

The short version: Wonpal operates entirely on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers. The app has no internet permission.

Overview

Wonpal is developed by Bleelblep. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our Android application designed for Nothing Phone devices.

Wonpal displays caller information on your Nothing Phone's Glyph Matrix LED when VoIP calls come in. It monitors incoming call notifications, matches caller names to your contacts, and renders the result on the Glyph LED interface.

Information We Access

To provide its functionality, Wonpal accesses certain device information locally:

Notifications

  • Wonpal uses Android's Notification Listener Service to detect incoming VoIP call notifications from apps you've selected (e.g. WhatsApp, Telegram, Discord).
  • We read the caller name from the notification to display it on the Glyph Matrix and match it to your contacts.
  • We read the notification category and flags to determine if the notification represents an incoming call and whether it is actively ringing.
  • We do not read message content, chat history, or unrelated notifications from apps you haven't enabled.

Contacts

  • If you grant the optional Contacts permission, Wonpal matches caller names from notifications to your device contacts.
  • This enables per-contact customization — custom display text, fonts, icons, and VIP status.
  • Contact data is only queried locally and is never transmitted off your device.
  • You can use Wonpal without this permission; caller names will still be displayed from notification data.

Phone State

  • We detect the current Do Not Disturb mode to respect your focus settings.
  • We do NOT access call logs, phone numbers, or call history from the system dialer.

Installed Applications

  • We query your list of installed applications to let you choose which calling apps Wonpal should monitor.
  • We also check which apps register as telecom providers to warn you about potential conflicts with Nothing OS's built-in call animations.
  • This information is used solely for the app selection interface and is stored locally on your device.

Information Storage

All data is stored locally on your device:

  • Preferences: Your app settings (brightness, scroll speed, theme, DND behavior, etc.) are stored in local DataStore preferences.
  • Database: Per-contact display configurations, VIP status, and call history entries are stored in a local Room database.
  • Call History: Wonpal maintains its own local call log of detected VoIP calls (caller name, source app, timestamp). This is separate from your system call history and is stored only on your device.

None of this data is transmitted to external servers.

Information We Do NOT Collect

Personal identification Location data Calendar Camera or microphone SMS messages System call logs Browsing history Device identifiers Analytics or usage stats Message or chat content Network activity Phone numbers

Third-Party Services

Wonpal does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app does not contain ads and has no internet access.

The app uses the Nothing Glyph SDK solely to control the Glyph LED matrix on compatible Nothing Phone devices. This SDK operates locally and does not transmit data externally.

Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, your information is protected by your device's built-in security features, including device encryption and app sandboxing.

You can delete all stored data at any time by clearing the app's data in Android Settings, or by using the in-app history clear function.

Children's Privacy

Wonpal does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect personal data from any users, children can safely use the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
