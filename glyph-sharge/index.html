Glyph Sharge

a quiet control centre for the lights on the back of your phone.

shake for battery. guard the usb port. swap the unlock animation. let the phone rest at night. seven small utilities, all toggleable, all running on nothing's official glyph api.

v1.0.24 · glow gate material you stable

what is it

Glyph Sharge turns the nothing phone's glyph leds into a proper control surface. it watches your battery, guards your usb port, runs unlock animations, and schedules quiet time — all behind a material-you interface that follows your wallpaper palette.

nothing runs in the background secretly. every feature is a toggle. pick the ones you want, leave the rest off.

seven knobs to turn

features, all toggleable

⚡

Power Peek

shake the phone to flash the battery % on the glyphs. adjustable sensitivity.

🛡

Glyph Guard

usb theft alert — unplug without permission and the phone lights up loud.

🔓

Glow Gate

custom unlock animation, rendered on the matrix every time you come back.

🔔

Low Battery Alert

threshold-based warnings before your phone taps out. set it and forget it.

📈

Battery Story

charging sessions visualised. see how you charge, how long, and how healthy.

🌙

Quiet Hours

scheduled glyph-off window. the phone rests while you rest.

💡

Glyph Service

start, stop, or reconfigure the underlying service without leaving the app.

why this one

what makes it different

  • 01 Official API, no hacks. uses nothing's sanctioned glyph api key — no debug mode, no root, no reverse engineering. it plays nicely with the system.
  • 02 Real utility, not just lights. usb theft guard and low-battery alerts solve actual problems, not just decoration. the rest is a nice bonus.
  • 03 Material You through and through. follows your wallpaper palette, both light and dark themes, three font options — system, Ndot, Ntype.
  • 04 Everything is a toggle. if you only want power peek, only power peek runs. nothing is forced on you.
heads up: glyph sharge does not support the phone (3) — the glyph matrix on that device works differently. if you're on a phone (3), try glyphcade or pixlings instead.
