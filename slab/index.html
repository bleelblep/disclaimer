§ 01 · What is slab

A floating edge.

A sidebar that slides out from the edge of the screen. It holds favourite apps, quick toggles, and small tools — without cluttering the home screen or eating notification-shade real estate.

  • Apps Pinned shortcuts, launched in floating or fullscreen mode.
  • Toggles Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, flashlight, rotation, more.
  • Tools Clipboard history, quick notes, small utilities.
  • Theme Bauhaus — paper and ink, three colours, no shadows.
