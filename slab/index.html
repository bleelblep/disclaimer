what is slab (｡•ᴗ•｡)

a floating sidebar that slides out from the edge of your screen. it holds your favorite apps, quick toggles, and small tools — without cluttering your home screen or eating notification shade real estate.

  • apps — pinned shortcuts, launched in floating or fullscreen mode
  • toggles — wifi, bluetooth, flashlight, rotation, and more
  • tools — clipboard history, quick notes, small utilities
  • cute theme — because why not

what it isn't ( ˃ ⌑ ˂ )

  • finished. some tabs are placeholders.
  • on the play store. this is a direct apk, distributed as-is.
  • guaranteed to work on every rom or android version.

requirements ٩(ˊᗜˋ*)و

  • android 10 or newer recommended
  • permission to draw over other apps (slab is an overlay)
  • accessibility permission for certain tools (optional)
  • a little patience ~
